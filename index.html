✏️ Keeping this up to date
Same idea as the HK version that inspired it. The exact dates and prices here are generated examples, not a live feed — each destination has a starting base fare near the top of the script, and the calendar of dates around it is auto-spread from that number. To update a route, find it in the DESTINATIONS list and change its base price (and hot: true to flag it as a flash deal). Every date chip links straight to a pre-filled Google Flights search so you can verify the real price before booking.
