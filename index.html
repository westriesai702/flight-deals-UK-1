✏️ How this works now
Tap a destination to load its real cached one-way fares from Aviasales (via the Travelpayouts Data API), shown per day across the next 6 months. These are genuine prices other travellers recently searched for — not invented numbers — but the cache can be up to about a week old, so a chip's price is a strong indicator, not a live quote. Each chip is one-way; tap it to open Google Flights on that date and pick your own return. If a route has no cached fares right now, you'll see a direct link to check Google Flights instead. To add or remove destinations, edit the DESTINATIONS list near the top of the script — no price numbers to maintain by hand anymore, since fares are fetched live.
